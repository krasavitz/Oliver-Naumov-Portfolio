Tchpack

Co-Founder, Designer, & Developer

01.25 - Present

Tchpack is a web application that revolutionizes how fashion designers connect with manufacturers. Using AI, the platform analyzes technical design photos/descriptions and automatically matches designers with the most suitable manufacturers based on capabilities, budget, and experience.

Problem

The fashion industry faces a significant challenge in connecting designers with manufacturers. Fashion designers and brands often spend months searching for the right manufacturers, with no reliable way to verify their capabilities or match their budgets. This leads to wasted time, production errors, and frustrated designers, particularly those new to the industry who lack established connections. The traditional process of finding manufacturers involves endless emails, uncertain outcomes, and significant time investment with no guaranteed results.

Role

As a Lead Designer and Contributing Developer at Tchpack, I took ownership of the entire design process and contributed to development. My primary focus was designing the complete user experience and interface, creating an intuitive tech pack upload flow, and developing the AI analysis results visualization. I established the design system, brand identity, and created responsive mobile-first layouts. On the development side, I contributed to frontend implementation using React, built components for consistent UI, and assisted with testing and refinement.

Solution

We designed an AI-powered platform that revolutionizes how designers connect with manufacturers. The system automatically analyzes uploaded tech pack photos and specifications, using AI to assess production requirements and complexity. The platform then matches designers with manufacturers based on specific criteria including production capabilities, budget alignment, previous experience, and quality standards. This creates a streamlined, efficient process that takes the guesswork out of manufacturer selection and provides clear, actionable recommendations.
